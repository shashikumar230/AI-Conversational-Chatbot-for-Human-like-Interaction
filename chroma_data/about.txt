1.National Skill Training Institute Bengaluru (NSTI Bengaluru)-- NSTI Bengaluru functions under the Directorate General of Training, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India. The institute is one of India's major training centers for vocational instructors and skill development programs. It is located at Yeshwanthpur, Bengaluru, Karnataka.
